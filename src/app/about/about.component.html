OUR CHURCH




Wisdom Chapel is a vibrant, multi-ethnic church with the intent to save lives and souls
We believe in the power of God’s Word to change lives and value the practical application
of His Word to make a difference in who we are and what we do. Believing and obeying His message
lead us in a discovery of real life and a hope for eternity. This is what the Bible teaches us,
that Jesus Christ came as God’s gift to the world with a message of good news.
